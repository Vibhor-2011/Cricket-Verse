Virat Kohli - Career

Virat Kohli is one of the greatest cricketers of the modern era. He made his international debut in 2008 and has represented India in Test, ODI, and T20 International cricket. Known for his consistency, aggressive batting style, and outstanding fitness, he has scored thousands of runs across all formats.

Achievements

  • 🏆 ICC Cricket World Cup winner
  • 🏏 Hundreds in international cricket
  • ⭐ Former captain of the Indian cricket team
  • 💪 One of the fittest athletes in world cricket

Why Fans Love Virat Kohli

His passion, determination, and match-winning performances have made him one of the most popular cricketers in the world.
